Botanical Characteristics:

  • Scientific name: Strobilanthes dyerianus.
  • It is a tropical evergreen shrub known for its striking, iridescent purple leaves.
  • The leaves are lance-shaped with green, silver, and purple metallic hues.
  • It typically grows 3 to 4 feet tall in ideal outdoor conditions.

Varieties:

  • The most common variety is Strobilanthes dyerianus, the classic Persian Shield.
  • Some cultivars may exhibit slightly varied leaf color intensities or growth habits.
  • Hybrid varieties are occasionally developed for improved color or compactness.

Benefits:

  • Primarily grown for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant foliage.
  • Excellent for adding color contrast to gardens, patios, or indoor spaces.
  • Attracts minimal pests, making it low-maintenance compared to other tropical plants.
  • Can be used as a temporary summer bedding plant in cooler climates.
