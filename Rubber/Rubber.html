Know about me

SCIENTIFIC NAME
Ficus Elastica
COMMON NAME
Rubber plant
HEIGHT
6-10 feet
WIDTH
3 meters
LEAVES COLOR
Dark green, deep maron or marked with yellow,cream,pink or white.
FLOWERS COLOR
green

Botanical Characteristics:

  • It is an evergreen plant with large, glossy, oval-shaped leaves.
  • Can grow both indoors and outdoors, reaching up to 6-10 feet indoors.
  • Native to South and Southeast Asia, commonly grown as an ornamental houseplant.

Varieties:

  • Ficus elastica 'Robusta' – Known for its large, dark green leaves.
  • Ficus elastica 'Tineke' – Features attractive green leaves with creamy white variegation.
  • Ficus elastica 'Burgundy' – Has deep reddish-black foliage, adding a dramatic look.

Benefits:

  • Acts as a natural air purifier, removing toxins like formaldehyde.
  • Improves indoor humidity levels.
  • Low maintenance, making it ideal for beginners.
  • Adds aesthetic appeal and greenery to homes and offices.

Care Instructions:

  • Prefers bright, indirect sunlight but tolerates moderate light.
  • Water when the top inch of soil feels dry; avoid overwatering.
  • Wipe leaves regularly to keep them dust-free and shiny.
  • Use well-draining potting soil and ensure proper drainage.
