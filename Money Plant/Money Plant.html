Know about me

SCIENTIFIC NAME
Epipremnum pinnatum cv
COMMON NAME
Golden pothos, money plant, or devil's ivy
HEIGHT
7-12 feet
WIDTH
22-38 cm
LEAVES COLOR
green in color

Botanical Characteristics:

  • Scientific name: Epipremnum aureum (commonly known as Money Plant or Devil's Ivy).
  • It is a fast-growing, evergreen, vine-like plant with heart-shaped, glossy green leaves.
  • Can grow in both soil and water, making it highly adaptable for indoor environments.
  • Known for its air-purifying properties as per NASA's clean air study.

Varieties:

  • Golden Money Plant – Features bright green leaves with golden-yellow variegation.
  • Marble Queen Money Plant – White and green marbled leaves, ideal for decorative purposes.
  • Neon Money Plant – Bright, neon-green leaves that add a vibrant touch to spaces.
  • Jade Money Plant – Dark green leaves with minimal variegation, popular in home gardens.

Benefits:

  • Believed to attract prosperity, wealth, and positive energy according to Vastu and Feng Shui.
  • Acts as a natural air purifier by removing toxins like formaldehyde and benzene.
  • Enhances indoor aesthetics with its trailing vines and lush green appearance.
  • Easy to propagate, making it cost-effective for gifting or expanding plant collection.

Care Instructions:

  • Requires indirect sunlight; avoid harsh, direct sun to prevent leaf burn.
  • Can be grown in both water and well-drained soil; change water regularly if grown hydroponically.
  • Water moderately; allow the topsoil to dry between watering to prevent root rot.
  • Occasional pruning helps maintain shape and encourages bushier growth.
