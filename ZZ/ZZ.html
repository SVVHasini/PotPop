Botanical Characteristics:

  • Scientific name: Zamioculcas zamiifolia, commonly known as ZZ Plant or Zanzibar Gem.
  • Features thick, glossy, dark green leaves that grow in a graceful, wand-like shape.
  • Grows from rhizomes, which store water, making it drought-tolerant.
  • Typically grows up to 2 to 3 feet indoors, with slow to moderate growth.

Varieties:

  • Classic Green ZZ – The most common variety with dark green, shiny leaves.
  • Raven ZZ – Features striking deep purple to nearly black foliage.
  • Dwarf ZZ (Zenzi) – A compact version with tightly packed, smaller leaves.
  • Variegated ZZ – Rare variety with cream or yellow patterns on the leaves.

Benefits:

  • Excellent air-purifying plant; removes toxins like xylene and toluene.
  • Requires minimal care, ideal for busy individuals or beginners.
  • Adds a lush, decorative look to homes and offices.
  • Believed to bring good luck and prosperity in Feng Shui.

Care Instructions:

  • Thrives in low to bright indirect light; avoid direct sun.
  • Water only when the soil is completely dry; overwatering can cause root rot.
  • Prefers well-draining soil and occasional wiping of leaves to maintain shine.
  • Can tolerate neglect but grows better with occasional feeding during the growing season.
