Know about me

SCIENTIFIC NAME
Dracaena Trifasciata
COMMON NAME
Snake Plant
HEIGHT
1 to 3 feet
WIDTH
6 to 36 inches
LEAVES COLOR
Gray/Silver
FLOWERS COLOR
White

Botanical Characteristics:

  • The plant features long, sword-shaped leaves with distinctive variegated patterns, often with yellow or light green edges, depending on the variety.
  • It can grow upright to 3 to 4 feet indoors, with some varieties reaching up to 6 feet under ideal conditions.
  • The plant occasionally produces small, fragrant, white to greenish flowers, though flowering is rare indoors.

Varieties:

  • Sansevieria trifasciata 'Laurentii' – Features yellow margins along the leaves.
  • Sansevieria trifasciata 'Hahnii' – A compact, bird's nest form ideal for desktops.
  • Sansevieria cylindrica – Has round, tubular leaves offering a unique architectural look.

Benefits:

  • Air Purification
  • Low Maintenance
  • Drought Tolerant

Care Instructions:

  • Light: Prefers bright, indirect light but can survive in low-light corners.
  • Watering: Allow soil to dry completely between waterings. Overwatering is the primary cause of plant death.
  • Soil: Requires well-draining soil, ideally a cactus or succulent mix.
  • Temperature: Prefers 18°C to 27°C (65°F to 80°F). Avoid cold drafts.
  • Fertilizer: Feed with diluted cactus fertilizer once a month during growing seasons (spring & summer).
