Botanical Characteristics:

  • Scientific Name: Aglaonema
  • It is a slow-growing, evergreen perennial with broad, glossy leaves.
  • Leaves are often variegated with silver, green, or cream patterns.
  • It is a popular indoor foliage plant due to its tolerance for low light.

Varieties:

  • Aglaonema 'Silver Bay' – Features large green leaves with silver centers.
  • Aglaonema 'Red Siam' – Known for its striking red-tinted leaves.
  • Aglaonema 'Maria' – Compact variety with dark green leaves and silver markings.
  • Over 20+ varieties exist with varying leaf colors and patterns.

Benefits:

  • Excellent air-purifying plant that removes indoor toxins.
  • Requires minimal maintenance, making it ideal for beginners.
  • Adds aesthetic appeal to homes and offices with its attractive foliage.
  • Believed to bring good luck and positive energy in some cultures.

Care Instructions:

  • Thrives in low to medium indirect light; avoid direct sunlight.
  • Water when the top inch of soil feels dry; do not overwater.
  • Prefers well-draining soil and occasional misting for humidity.
  • Tolerates average indoor temperatures but avoid cold drafts.
