Know about me

SCIENTIFIC NAME
Zamioculcas Zamiifolia
COMMON NAME
ZZ plant
HEIGHT
2-3 feet
WIDTH
3 feet
LEAVES COLOR
Bright green
FLOWERS COLOR
bright yellow to brown or bronze

Botanical Characteristics:

  • Scientific name: Chlorophytum comosum.
  • It is a fast-growing, evergreen perennial herb.
  • Features long, arching, narrow leaves that are usually green with white or yellow stripes.
  • Produces small white flowers on long stems, followed by plantlets or "baby spiders."

Varieties:

  • Variegatum: Green leaves with creamy-white edges (most common).
  • Vittatum: Green leaves with a broad white stripe down the center.
  • Bonnie: A curly-leaved variety, often with similar variegation to Vittatum.
  • Solid Green: Entirely green leaves, less common but available.

Benefits:

  • Excellent indoor air purifier; removes toxins like formaldehyde and carbon monoxide.
  • Non-toxic to pets, making it a safe choice for homes with animals.
  • Improves humidity levels, reducing the risk of respiratory issues.
  • Easy to propagate, providing free new plants through its plantlets.

Care Instructions:

  • Prefers bright, indirect sunlight but can tolerate low-light conditions.
  • Water moderately; allow the soil to dry slightly between waterings.
  • Grows well in hanging baskets or pots with well-draining soil.
  • Requires occasional pruning of dead leaves and plantlets for better appearance.
