Know about me

SCIENTIFIC NAME
Spathiphyllum
COMMON NAME
Peace lily
HEIGHT
1-4 feet
WIDTH
5 inches
LEAVES COLOR
Emerald green
FLOWERS COLOR
White

Botanical Characteristics:

  • Scientific name: Spathiphyllum species, belonging to the Araceae family.
  • Known for its glossy, dark green leaves and elegant white spathes (often mistaken for flowers).
  • It is a perennial, low-growing plant ideal for indoor environments.
  • Prefers indirect sunlight and thrives well in shaded areas.

Varieties:

  • Spathiphyllum 'Mauna Loa' – Popular large variety with broad leaves.
  • Spathiphyllum 'Wallisii' – Compact variety, suitable for desks and small spaces.
  • Spathiphyllum 'Sensation' – Largest cultivar, with dramatic foliage.
  • Spathiphyllum 'Domino' – Unique variety with variegated, white-streaked leaves.

Benefits:

  • Acts as a natural air purifier by removing toxins like benzene, formaldehyde, and carbon monoxide.
  • Enhances indoor décor with its graceful appearance.
  • Low-maintenance and easy to grow, making it ideal for beginners.
  • Helps increase indoor humidity, which can benefit respiratory health.

Care Instructions:

  • Keep in bright, indirect light; avoid direct sunlight to prevent leaf burn.
  • Water once the topsoil feels dry; avoid overwatering to prevent root rot.
  • Wipe leaves occasionally to remove dust and allow better respiration.
  • Fertilize monthly during the growing season (spring and summer) with a balanced liquid fertilizer.
